Research Interests

I am a researcher in theoretical and computational astrophysics. In particular I am interested in magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) and plasma physics. During my Ph.D. studies I have mainly focused on dynamo theories, that is, how magnetic fields can be amplifed from some seed field in plasmas. Later on I picked up the skill of doing numerical simulations (the Pencil Code) during my postdoc years, and extended my area of interest to include MHD-related processes in planet atmospheres, accretion disks, and the early Universe.
